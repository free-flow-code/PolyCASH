## Weather module

### Получение погодных рынков

Поиск "температурных" рынков происходит по id тега `Daily Temperature`. В ответ получаем 8 видов рынков:
- `Will the lowest temperature in Miami be 65°F or below on April 26?`
- `Will the lowest temperature in Miami be between 66-67°F on April 26?`
- `Will the lowest temperature in Miami be 14°C on April 26?`
- `Will the lowest temperature in Miami be 84°F or higher on April 26?`
- `Will the highest temperature in Miami be 65°F or below on April 26?`
- `Will the highest temperature in Miami be between 66-67°F on April 26?`
- `Will the highest temperature in Miami be 14°C on April 26?`
- `Will the highest temperature in Miami be 84°F or higher on April 26?`
